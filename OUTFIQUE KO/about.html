About Outfique

Outfique is a smart wardrobe web app designed to help users store clothes, organize items by category, save outfit combinations, and get style inspiration based on their personal preferences.

What the app does

  • Lets users sign up and log in securely
  • Stores wardrobe items under Hat, Shirt, Pants, and Boots
  • Supports camera capture for saving clothes into wardrobe drawers
  • Allows daily outfit building and saving
  • Generates outfit ideas based on your saved style preferences

Why it matters

Instead of keeping fashion ideas scattered across multiple apps, Outfique gives you one place to manage clothing pieces and make outfit planning easier, faster, and more personal to your style.

Features

  • Smart Organization: Automatically organize clothes by category
  • Daily Outfit Builder: Create and save complete outfit combinations
  • Camera Capture: Photograph clothes to add them to your wardrobe
  • Style Personalization: Customize item descriptions with your own notes
  • Editable Items: Update style and description of any saved item
